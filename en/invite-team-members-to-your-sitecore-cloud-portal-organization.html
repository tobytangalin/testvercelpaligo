Invite team members to your Sitecore Cloud Portal organization

Abstract

Invite team members to your organization and apps on Sitecore Cloud.

When your Sitecore Cloud Portal organization has been set up, you can invite team members and give them access to your organization's apps.

A team member is anyone that is a part of the organization. A team member's organization role and app roles determine the apps they can open and the work they can perform. For information about the different types of team members, see roles.

Note

To invite a team member to an organization, you must have an Organization Admin or Organization Owner role.

To invite a team member to your Sitecore Cloud Portal organization:

  1. Log in to the Sitecore Cloud Portal.

  2. If you are a member of multiple organizations, you must select the appropriate organization.

  3. On the homepage, on the menu bar, click Admin, then on the Team members page, click Invite.

  4. In the Invite team members dialog box, in the Email addresses field, enter the email address of the team member that you want to invite.

    Note

    If your organization has an enabled SSO connection, you can only send invitations to email domains that belong to one of your organization's enabled SSO connections.

    You can invite up to a hundred members at a time. To enter multiple email addresses, separate them with a comma, semicolon, or a space.

  5. In the Organization access drop-down menu, to select the level of access that you want this member to have, click either Admin, User, or Owner. To assign the Organization Owner role, you must be an Organization Owner.

    Organization role

    Description

    Organization User

    • Only has access to the apps that an Organization Admin or an Organization Owner assigns to them.

    • Can be assigned up to 25 apps.

    Organization Admin

    Has an Admin role in all apps in the organization. They can also:

    • Invite team members to the organization, manage their access to each app, and export their data.

    • Manage SSO connections.

    • Manage MFA for your organization.

    • Access the XM Cloud Deploy app.

    • Create a support case.

    Organization Owner

    Has the same permissions and access as an Organization Admin. They can also:

    • Assign and remove other Organization Owners.

    Note

    An organization can have multiple Organization Owners.

  6. If you select User, you can give the team member access to an app.

    • To give a team member access to an app, click Add app, then in the App access drop-down menus, click the relevant app and the level of access you want them to have in this app.

    • To give a team member access to multiple apps, click Add app and select the app and access level again.

    • If you have not deployed an app yet, you must update the access rights of the team member after you deploy an app.

  7. Click Send.

    An email message is sent to the team member informing them they have been invited to join your Sitecore Cloud Portal organization. You can manage your invitations on the Invitations page.
