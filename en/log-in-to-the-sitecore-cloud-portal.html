Log in to the Sitecore Cloud Portal

Abstract

Describes how to log in and out of the Sitecore Cloud Portal.

To access your Sitecore apps, you must first log in to the Sitecore Cloud Portal.

To log in to the Sitecore Cloud Portal:

  1. Go to https://portal.sitecorecloud.io/.

  2. On the login page, enter the email address associated with your Sitecore Cloud Portal account, then click Continue.

  3. If your organization uses the default Sitecore authentication, enter your password, then click Continue.

    Alternatively, if your organization uses single sign-on (SSO), you will be redirected to the sign in page of your organization's identity provider. Sign in using your credentials.

To log out of the Sitecore Cloud Portal:

  • In the top-right of the menu bar, click your Profile , then click Log out.
