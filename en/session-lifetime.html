Session lifetime

Abstract

Describes the Sitecore Cloud Portal's session timeout and limitations.

When a team member logs in to the Sitecore Cloud Portal or a Sitecore app, they are automatically logged out after 10 hours of inactivity.

If a team member logs in using SSO, the inactivity timeout is 10 hours even if the identity provider (IdP) inactivity timeout is less than 10 hours.

If the IdP inactivity timeout is more than 10 hours, a team member must log in again after 10 hours of inactivity. However, if they log in again before the IdP inactivity timeout is reached, they don't need to enter their password.

The default Sitecore authentication uses non-persistent sessions. This means that in most cases, if you close your web browser, you must log in again.

Web browser limitations

In some cases, non-persistent sessions cannot be enforced.

Examples include:

  • Your browser has a session restore setting enabled. Restoring the session also restores the session cookie.

  • You close your Sitecore app tab but not the browser window.

In these cases, the idle session timeout is still 10 hours.
