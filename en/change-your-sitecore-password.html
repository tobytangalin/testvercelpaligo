Change your Sitecore password

Abstract

How to change your Sitecore password in the Sitecore Cloud Portal.

Note

If you log in to the Sitecore Cloud Portal using single sign-on (SSO), you can only reset your password through your identity provider.

If you log in to the Sitecore Cloud Portal using the default Sitecore authentication, you can change your Sitecore password from the Sitecore Cloud Portal.

Note

If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it from the login page.

To change your Sitecore password:

  1. Log in to the Sitecore Cloud Portal, and on the top-right corner of the menu bar, click the circle that shows your initials, then click Manage account.

  2. On your account page, click Reset password. Sitecore will send a password reset email to the email address associated with your account.

  3. Go to the email and click Set a new password.

    Note

    If you didn't receive an email, check your junk or spam folder.

  4. In the New password field and the Re-enter new password field, enter the password you want, then click Reset password.

    Note

    Your password must be at least 8 characters and contain at least 3 of the following:

    • Uppercase letters (A-Z)

    • Lowercase letters (a-z)

    • Base 10 digit (0 - 9)

    • Non-alphanumeric characters such as ! @ # $ % ^ & *
