Export team member data

Abstract

Describes how to export the team member data of an organization.

You can export information on team members in the Sitecore Cloud Portal. The export generates a CSV file containing the name, email address, access, and last login date of every team member in your organization.

To export team member data:

  1. In the Sitecore Cloud Portal, on the menu bar, click Admin.

  2. Click Export. The CSV file automatically downloads.
